Cleaning Tips

At Maids Unlimited we know that keeping a house clean and tidy can be challenging. Here are a few helpful tips that you can do in between cleanings to help with this dirty little task.

  • Inspect and Change Air Filters
  • Clean Garbage Disposal
  • Wash Exterior Windows and Siding
  • Unclog Drains
  • Wipe down counters regularly
  • Take care of the mail
  • Keep dishes in the dishwasher or cabinets
