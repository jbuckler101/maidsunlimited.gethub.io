About the Owner

As a working Mom, in the midst of homework, sports schedules, and birthday parties, Carrie struggled with keeping the house clean and keeping up with basic chores around the house. Once her daughters grew up Carrie decided that she wanted to help other families by taking that struggle off of their plate. She is a small business owner who is determined to help you keep your house clean and free up some time for you.
